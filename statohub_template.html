The explanation

Standard deviation

It measures how spread out a set of numbers is around their mean. A small value means the data clusters tightly; a large value means it's scattered widely.

For a full population you divide by n; for a sample, by n−1.

σ = √( Σ(xᵢ − μ)² / n )
